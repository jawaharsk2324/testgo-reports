Methodology

Nucleic acids are extracted from urine samples and analyzed using Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR). The Genepath-MGN-LUTI assays detect urinary tract pathogens and antibiotic resistance markers with greater than 99% accuracy and specificity. Infection rangesclassified as high, medium, or lowwere established during method validation based on corresponding CFU/L values. The clinical significance of these ranges has not been evaluated by MicroGen.
