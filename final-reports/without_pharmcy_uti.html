Limitation

Absence of detection does not rule out the presence of other microorganisms or the possibility that the target sequence exists below the limit of detection. This UTI Diagnostic Report does not consider patient history, potential drug interactions, drug sensitivities, or allergies. It is the responsibility of the treating physician to determine the appropriate medication and dosing based on the complete clinical context and all available data.

Methodology

Nucleic acids are extracted from urine samples and analyzed using Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR). The Genepath-MGN-LUTI assays detect urinary tract pathogens and antibiotic resistance markers with greater than 99% accuracy and specificity. Infection ranges—classified as high, medium, or low—were established during method validation based on corresponding CFU/μL values. The clinical significance of these ranges has not been evaluated by TestGo - Chantilly.
