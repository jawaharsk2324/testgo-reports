Methodology

Nucleic acids are extracted from urine samples and analyzed using Real-Time Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR). The Genepath-MGN-LSTI assays detect urinary tract pathogens and antibiotic resistance markers with greater than 99% accuracy and specificity. Infection ranges—classified as high, medium, or low—were established during method validation based on corresponding CFU/μL values. The clinical significance of these ranges has not been evaluated by TestGo - Irving.
